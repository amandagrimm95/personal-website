About Me

My name is Amanda Grimm. I originally went to school for Multidisciplinary Engineering and Technical Theatre with the goal of working in live entertainment and amusement park design. I was fortunate enough to fulfil that dream, but when the COVID-19 pandemic hit the entertainment industry started to slow down. I used that time to discover a new passion, coding. I have always enjoyed creating tools to increase efficiency and help with more repetitive tasks, and I am excited to shift into this industry and continue to explore this passion.

I graduated from Purdue University in 2018 and moved to Upstate NY with my now fiance. Since then we have really made a home for ourselves in New York. We love being surrounded by the beautiful mountains, a nice change of pace for a couple of kids from the flat mid-west.

In our free time we love to play games of all kinds, from board games with local friends to video games with friends and family accorss the country. I also love to do anything that allows me to work with my hands and use some creativity including baking, crocheting, drawing, and painting.
